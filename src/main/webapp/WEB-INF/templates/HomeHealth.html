Home Health

Astra Healthcare provides a dependable and convenient service of blood tests at your home/office. It is not just a service but a comforting privilege in which medically equipped crew members attend to you at your residence and collect the required test samples. Suburban Diagnostics observes high standards of practice and is proud to extend this convenience to our clients.

Just call our Health Line to avail of our world class diagnostic services right at your door step.

You can now book your blood test at home or at your office: Please give us a call or fill the online form to request for an appointment When you call for your appointment please give us your detailed name, address, telephone number along with the list of tests which you are required to undertake. Providing complete test details will help us inform you of any special test preparation such as fasting etc. which may be required.

Our technician will visit you for collection of the blood sample which is transported safely to our laboratory for processing.

The test results can be emailed to you, sent to your home/office / doctor or even downloaded from our website for convenience.
